A VARITERY OF SONGS FOR YOUR EVENT

We provide an experience beyond what a typical wedding or cover band can offer because we’re just as proficient in performing your guest’s favorite songs as we represent Baton Rouge internationally with our original music at festivals and venues. Please let us know when booking if there are any specific songs on the list that must be played and/or avoided. We’ll gladly perform up to two requests not on this list, free of charge.
